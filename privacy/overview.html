Trust

Earning your trust drives us every day. We've designed our privacy policy to explain how we manage your personal information.



Security

We regularly review and update our security measures and provide options to help further safeguard your information.



Choice

We realize that everyone is different. That's why we give you options when it comes to your privacy preferences.

A range of privacy resources for you to explore

Customers of our retail brokerage or mutual fund businesses can review our privacy policy and privacy notice:

  • Privacy policy: Our privacy policy provides information about how we collect, use, and share your personal information, including our privacy notice for California residents, as required by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
  • Privacy notice: This notice is intended for individuals who seek, apply for, or obtain our financial products and services for personal or household purposes and is provided in accordance with federal law.

Additionally, you can review and exercise privacy choices and access privacy FAQs and other helpful resources.

Please note that if you have relationships with other Fidelity businesses, they may have a different privacy policy that applies when you engage with that business. We may also offer products and services that may have a different or additional privacy policy that pertains to those products and services.

Take control of your security

To protect your Fidelity accounts and your information, we use measures such as strong encryption, firewalls, secure email, and proactive 24/7 system surveillance. We also provide extra security measures for the ways you like to do business with us, including multi-factor authentication, money transfer lockdown, security text alerts, and voice biometrics.

Learn more about our security measures and the steps you can take to further protect your accounts and information. Customers can also access their security settingsLog In Required to update their information and other security preferences.
