Let us help you make a difference

Giving back can have a multitude of benefits for you, your favorite charities, and those they serve. Knowing how to get started and make the most impact can be daunting. That's where we come in.

What we can help you do


Define your goals

Have a clear, set purpose for your philanthropy.


Maximize your impact

Make your dollars go farther while maximizing tax benefits.


Leave your mark

Create a lasting legacy and involve others.

Ways to work with us

The Fidelity Charitable® Giving Account®


Maximize your charitable impact and reduce taxes with Fidelity Charitable®, the nation's largest grantmaker and donor-advised fund sponsor.


  • Give cash, stocks, and more to qualify for an income tax deduction and reduce capital gains.
  • Invest the charitable balance for potential tax-free growth and recommend grants to charities over time.
  • No minimum to open, no ongoing balance required and cheaper than giving with a credit card.

Open a Giving Account


Learn more about Fidelity Charitable®

Fidelity Philanthropic Consulting


Bring your vision to life with customized philanthropic guidance, led by experienced consultants.


  • Whether you set up a donor-advised fund at Fidelity Charitable or use another giving vehicle, we can help develop or refresh a giving strategy that's aligned with your values.
  • We can identify and assess nonprofit organizations addressing those causes you care deeply about.
  • We can offer training to effectively engage your family or team in philanthropy.

Achieve greater impact

Linda and Tom were selling their business and entering retirement. Their advisor suggested contributing business interests to a donor-advised fund at Fidelity Charitable® for a tax-smart and efficient way to manage their windfall while potentially giving more to charity. After opening a Giving Account®, they were interested in finding a focus for their giving and engaging their adult children in philanthropy. They worked with Fidelity Philanthropic Consulting to identify their shared values and craft a family mission statement so they can make a difference together. Giving back is now one of Linda and Tom's greatest joys in this new phase of life.

Frequently asked questions

  • Expand all
  • Collapse all

  • What's the difference between the Fidelity Charitable® Giving Account® and Fidelity Philanthropic Consulting?

    The Fidelity Charitable® Giving Account® is like a charitable investment account. It serves as an alternative or complement to giving directly to charity or through other mediums like private foundations.

    Fidelity Philanthropic Consulting offers guidance and customized philanthropic support, from researching and evaluating nonprofit organizations to helping you set up a giving strategy.

    You can use these offerings together or choose one depending on your goals.

  • What' the benefit of using these offerings for my giving?

    Using these services provides you with expertise to give back in a more thoughtful way. We can help you potentially save more on taxes, think strategically about your giving, and ultimately make a bigger impact.

  • Do I need to have an existing Fidelity relationship?

    No, anyone can benefit from the services of Fidelity Philanthropic Consulting and Fidelity Charitable®.

  • Do you work with companies?

    Yes, Fidelity has a suite of solutions to help with corporate giving and employee workplace programs. Contact philconsulting@fmr.com to learn more.
